1. What is Eventswat.com all about? What information does it provide about a fest?

Eventswat.com is a consortium of all the college campus events in India. This includes both national and international Events. Eventswat.com is a one stop solution for accessing even minuscule of information about the events. Our aim is to connect students from campuses all over India by making them aware of Technical, Cultural, Management Fests, Workshops, Conferences, Seminars organized by each and every college in India. EVentswat.com provides details about the fests, festivals by type or festivals in a month, festivals in a state.

2. How many colleges in India do you cover?

Eventswat.com has tie-ups with 700 Colleges across India with 1500 Eventswat.com Student Partners. Eventswat.com has covered more than 5000 events in the past 5 years.

3. Please share with us the inspiration and the story behind your start-up?

On the personal front, I never had an opportunity to know about and participate in various college fests throughout the country. After an initial survey, I understood its not only me but my friends as well who had no awareness about the college festivals. In fact, I had to visit the respective college fest website to get myself updated, which was very time consuming. Finding appropriate solutions was the only inevitable thing I could do the result was Eventswat.com . I can now proudly say that I have successfully launched Indias first and leading website for campus festivals.
